- ID & Photos
- Chargers & Cables
- Power Bank

- Sunscreen
- Toothbrush & Paste

- Fleece Jacket
- Gloves
- Beanie & Cap
- Hiking Shoes
- Handkerchief
- Pairs of Socks
- Poncho
